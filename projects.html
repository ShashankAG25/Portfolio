Signature Segmentation from document images

Developed a project focused on signature detection and segmentation from scanned document images. Leveraged the SPODS dataset for training and testing the proposed method, achieved 83% accuracy. Comparative analysis of ResNet50, ResNet101, ResNet152v2 and YoloV5s are done.

Technologies Used

Python,ResNet50 model, OpenCV, TensorFlow, and Keras libraries
